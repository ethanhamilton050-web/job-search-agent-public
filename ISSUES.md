### C. Per-job wall-clock cap â€” the deeper half of the queue-freeze fix
Reach-form now fails fast (120 s unattended, log #5), but a job can still be slow
*inside* the wizard (~14 steps Ã— up to ~20 s waits each). Real fix = a whole-run

a subprocess), and `filler.fill_application()` runs synchronously in that same
process/thread â€” so a hard timeout can only be enforced by killing a whole

squarely **do WITH Ethan on the host, not blind.** Confirmed, not overturned.
